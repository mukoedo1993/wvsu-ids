AI workshop, WVSU




Artificial Intelligence Workshop: Exploring Data Mining and Image Analysis with deep learning

August 25th, 2023.

Introduction:
The utilization of artificial intelligence in agriculture is on the rise. This workshop serves two primary objectives: firstly, to demystify deep learning and facilitate an understanding of artificial intelligence; secondly, to equip participants with essential deep learning tools for their future research endeavors.The workshop comprises two sessions. The first session focuses on a long short-term memory model, centered around data mining and prediction. The second session centers on a convolutional model, emphasizing feature classification and recognition. Each session includes a practical project to enhance participants' mastery of the respective models.
